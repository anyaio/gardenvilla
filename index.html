What is Phuket Garden Villa?

Our villa is designed with harmony and comfort in mind. Located in Phuket, Thailand, a tropical paradise and convenient place for living, working and holidays. This property is available for sale and rent directly from the owners.

The spacious 2-story 345 m² building is located on a large 745 m² land plot with a spacious tropical garden, pool that is surrounded by a wooden deck.

This house features a comfortable design: 4 bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, airy rooms, western-style kitchen, open dinner and living rooms and a separate laundry rooms. There is a wooden sala (outdoor covered sitting area) and a covered parking lot for 2 cars on the premises.

This villa is conveniently located on secure territory of Tanode Villa 2 compound made up of only 11 villasThe Phuket Garden Villa is only 5 minutes away from one of the most beautiful beaches in Phuket, Layan Beach. It is only 10-15 minutes drive to Phuket International Academy (PIA) and British International School (BIS). Tesco Lotus shopping mall is within 5 minutes reach and Phuket International Airport 15 minutes drive only.

The house is perfect for a permanent stay, vacations and to be rented out.

Layan Beach, Phuket

Location

Layan Beach is a group of small beaches north of Bang Tao. It is a National Park with beautiful trees, white sand and crystal blue water away from the hustle and bustle making it the ideal place to relax and enjoy the surroundings in nature. Construction on or around Layan Beach is prohibited making it the ideal place to rejuvenate yourself with the sights and sounds of nature.

The surrounding area around the villas has developed over the years to feature restaurants, Kindergartens, convenient stores and entertainment centers making your stay more convenient and accessible. Within walking distance you can access the luxury resorts of Laguna where they offer horse and elephant riding. Exquisite restaurants and access to one of the best golfing experiences in Phuket, The Laguna Phuket Golf Club.

You are bound to fall in love with this place.
